Helicopter Oculus VR

Virtual Reality(VR) Helicopter Flight Game

Oculus VR game created with Unity3D, C#, and WRLD Unity SDK which generate 3D maps based on real-world coordinates.
Pilot your helicopter to POIs within the city with the help of temporary directional arrows or explore the city scape on your own.
This game stems from my graduate studies project which explored the use of temporarily visible 3D navigational aids.

Known Issue: WRLD API bug may cause a crash while playing the Toronto level.
